## Story

**As** the compliance lead using the AI partner,
**I want** a `gap_analysis` skill that turns the four diagnostic tools into one prioritised, action-attached Swedish report ‚Äî and the `assess_change` skill to read our *current* compliance posture (kravpunkter, linked styrdokument, prior assessments) before it judges a lag√§ndring,

This is the second skills-track story.
