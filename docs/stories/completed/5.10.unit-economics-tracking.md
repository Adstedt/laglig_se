# Story 5.10: Unit Economics ‚Äî Revenue, Margin & Weekly Founder Report (NFR18)

## Story

**As a** founder validating the business model,
**I want** per-workspace gross margin (revenue ‚àí cost) tracked monthly and reported to me weekly,
**so that** I can confirm gross margin stays >80% across tiers and catch any workspace that goes unprofitable.

## Acceptance Criteria

### Currency ‚Äî DECIDED: report in SEK

1. **All monetary values in the rollup are stored and reported in SEK** (the founder's accounting currency; tier prices are already SEK). `cost_usd_estimate` is in USD, so the rollup converts cost ‚Üí SEK via **one** documented `USD_TO_SEK` constant (single definition, flagged "verify FX periodically"). Revenue stays native SEK. Margin math therefore operates entirely in SEK ‚Äî never mix the two operands. (Decision locked by PO 2026-06-12; no longer a dev choice.)

### Monthly rollup (the missing data layer)

2. A `WorkspaceCost` (monthly rollup) table stores, per `(workspace_id, month)`: `ai_cost`, `infra_cost` (storage + vector, see AC 4), `total_cost`, `revenue`, `gross_margin_pct`, in the AC-1 currency. Unique on `(workspace_id, month)`.
3. **AI cost** is summed from existing `ChatUsageEvent.cost_usd_estimate` for the month (no new per-event logging). **Add `claude-opus-4-8` to the `PRICING` table in `cost-estimator.ts`** ‚Äî it is currently absent, so any Opus-4.8 path (e.g. `lib/agent/skills/generate-law-list.ts`) records `cost_usd_estimate = 0` and silently undercounts cost.
4. **Infra cost** = storage (`WorkspaceUsage.storage_bytes` √ó a documented per-GB-month rate) **plus** a documented flat per-workspace allocation for vector/DB, both defined as named constants in one place (see Dev Notes ‚Üí Cost constants). **Do NOT fabricate a per-query vector price** ‚Äî Supabase pgvector queries are not separately metered; a flat monthly allocation (stated as an explicit assumption) is the honest model. NFR18's "vector query costs" is satisfied by this allocation, not invented per-query pricing. Ship with the **provisional** values in Dev Notes; mark them `// PROVISIONAL ‚Äî confirm against real Supabase/Vercel invoice before trusting margins for decisions`.
5. **Revenue** is the tier's monthly price (Solo 499 / Team 1299 SEK; TRIAL 0). **ENTERPRISE revenue is not auto-derivable** (sales-led, per-contract) ‚Äî exclude ENTERPRISE workspaces from auto-margin or mark them "manual revenue entry required"; never hardcode an Enterprise price.

### Aggregation cron

6. A monthly Vercel cron (`/api/cron/aggregate-costs`, bearer-auth via `CRON_SECRET`, registered in `vercel.json`) upserts one `WorkspaceCost` row per active workspace for the prior month. `gross_margin_pct = revenue > 0 ? (revenue ‚àí total_cost) / revenue √ó 100 : null`.

### Reporting & alerting

7. A weekly founder email (cron, `sendHtmlEmail`, Resend) reports: avg gross margin % (target >80%), total MRR, total cost, and the count + list of workspaces under the alert threshold.
8. **Alerting:** any workspace with `gross_margin_pct < 60` and `revenue > 0` is flagged in the weekly email (and/or a log/Sentry breadcrumb). 60% is the alert floor; 80% is the target.
9. **Admin dashboard:** extend the existing `getUsageByWorkspace` / `usage-tables.tsx` surface with **revenue** and **gross-margin %** columns (cost + tier already render). Cohort view = margin grouped by tier (Solo / Team / Enterprise). No new dashboard page required ‚Äî augment the existing one.

### Non-functional

10. The aggregation cron completes within its `maxDuration` for the current workspace count; cost logging on the hot chat path is unchanged (already done in 14.27 ‚Äî this story adds no per-request work).
