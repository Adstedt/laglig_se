## Story

**As a** product owner,
**I want** the Miljö law list template fully populated with 98 documents across redesigned sections,
**so that** we have a second domain template demonstrating cross-domain patterns.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. `LawListTemplate` record created for Miljö domain
2. Section structure redesigned from source's 9 sections to fix two quality violations:
   - **Section 08 merge:** Source Section 08 (Brandskydd) has only 2 documents — below the 3-document minimum. Merge into Section 06 (Farliga ämnen och kemikalier) or a new combined section.
   - **Section 09 split:** Source Section 09 has 47 documents (48%) — exceeds the 30% "Other" threshold. Split into proposed sub-sections:
     - Avfallshantering och producentansvar (~8 docs)
     - Strålning och strålskydd (~3 docs)
     - Energi, klimat och utsläppshandel (~6 docs)
     - Seveso och farlig verksamhet (~4 docs)
     - EU-produktkrav och marknadskontroll (~8 docs)
     - Övriga specialbestämmelser (~18 docs remaining)
   - Final section count: 12-14 sections (from original 9)
3. 98 `TemplateItem` records with SSDD indexes, document links, source types
4. `is_service_company_relevant` flags set (30 of 98 = true, based on file 04 overlap)
5. AI content generation populates all summaries and commentary
6. Cross-list references populated (22 shared with Arbetsmiljö)
7. Quality checklist passes: no section < 3 docs, no section exceeds 30% of total, section names are original laglig.se Swedish
