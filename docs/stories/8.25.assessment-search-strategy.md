# Story 8.25: Improve Assessment Search Strategy with Section-Aware Queries

## Status

Draft

## Story

**As a** compliance officer reviewing an amendment assessment,
**I want** the AI to search for the specific sections being amended (not just broad keywords),
**so that** the assessment cites the actual legal text of the changed paragraphs and provides more precise analysis.

## Context & Dependencies

### The Problem

The assessment workflow tells the AI to call `search_laws` for "relevant lagtext för kontext" — but gives no guidance on *what* to search for. The AI generates broad keyword queries like "plan- och bygglagen bygglov detaljplan krav hantverkare" instead of targeted section lookups like "9 kap 80 § plan- och bygglagen."

This matters because the system prompt already provides:
- **Berörda paragrafer**: "Kap 9 § 80 (ändrad), Kap 9 § 97 (ändrad), ..." (from Story 8.22)
- **Amendment full text**: the actual new wording of those sections
- **Proposition context**: why the law was changed (from Story 8.24)

The AI has all the information to formulate precise queries, but the workflow instructions don't tell it to use the section references for search.

### Observed Behavior (SFS 2025:1079)

The AI searched:
1. "plan- och bygglagen bygglov lov förhandsbesked handläggning" — broad, generic
2. "Myndigheten för civilt försvar totalförsvar riksintresse bygglov" — better, but still keyword-based

Ideal searches would have been:
1. "9 kap 80 § plan- och bygglagen" — the specific section about defense authority consent
2. "9 kap 97 § plan- och bygglagen" — the notification obligation section
3. Or a query combining the affected sections with the base law

### Dependencies

- **Story 8.22** (completed) — provides "Berörda paragrafer" line in system prompt
- **Story 8.24** (completed) — provides proposition context in system prompt

### Scope

1. Update the `ASSESSMENT_WORKFLOW` instructions in `lib/agent/system-prompt.ts` to guide the AI toward section-specific searches
2. Optionally update `get_change_details` tool guidance to clarify when it should be called

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The `ASSESSMENT_WORKFLOW` instructions explicitly tell the AI to search for the specific affected sections listed in "Berörda paragrafer" using chapter and paragraph numbers
2. The workflow instructions distinguish between two search purposes: (a) retrieve the actual text of amended sections from the base law, and (b) search for related legal context
3. When section references are available, the AI's `search_laws` queries include specific paragraph references (e.g., "9 kap 80 § plan- och bygglagen") rather than only broad keywords
4. Assessment quality is maintained or improved — no regressions in output accuracy
5. All existing tests pass, `tsc` clean
