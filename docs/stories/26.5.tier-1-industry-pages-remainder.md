# Story 26.5: Tier-1 Industry Pages — Remainder (Fastighet, Vård & omsorg, Industri, Transport)

## Status

InProgress

## Story

**As a** Swedish SME in fastighet, vård & omsorg, industri/tillverkning or transport/logistik searching for which laws apply to my business,
**I want** an industry-specific, content-complete page that answers my question and shows the laglista solving it,
**so that** Laglig captures the remaining Tier-1 industry searches with the template 26.4 proved — at a fraction of 26.4's cost, because the engine, the playbook and the demo infrastructure already exist.
