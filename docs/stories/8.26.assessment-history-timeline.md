# Story 8.26: Assessment History Timeline & Audit Trail

## Status

Backlog

## Story

**As a** compliance officer reviewing a law on my law list,
**I want** to see all past amendment assessments for that law in chronological order,
**so that** I have a complete audit trail of how changes were reviewed and what decisions were made.

## Context

Assessment data already exists in the database — `ChangeAssessment` records (status, impact, notes) and `ChatMessage` history per change event. But once a change is acknowledged, it disappears from the UI with no way to revisit past assessments.

For compliance auditing, traceability, and institutional memory, users need to see: "this law changed 4 times in the last year, here's what we assessed and decided each time."

## Ideas

### Assessment History Timeline
- **Modal/panel on law list item**: "Visa historik" showing all past assessments as a timeline
- **Each entry**: amendment SFS, date, AI summary, status (Granskad/Åtgärd krävs/Ej tillämplig), impact level, who reviewed it, link to reopen the conversation
- **Agent tool**: `get_assessment_history` — lets the AI reference past assessments when reviewing a new change ("last time this law changed, you assessed it as low impact")

### Assessment Chat Persistence & Sharing
- **Chat history in sidebar/history view**: assessment conversations should appear in chat history, grouped separately from regular AI chats (e.g., "Bedömningar" section)
- **Revisit past conversations**: click a past assessment to reopen the full chat thread (read-only or continuable)
- **Sharing**: generate a shareable link or read-only view for team members — "here's what we concluded about SFS 2026:293"
- **Context for new assessments**: when reviewing a new amendment to the same law, the AI should be able to reference prior assessment conversations for continuity

### Export & Audit
- **Export**: PDF/CSV of assessment history for audit documentation
- **Compliance report**: "Here are all assessments made in Q1 2026, with status, impact, and who reviewed"

### Technical Notes
- `ChatMessage` records already exist with `context_type: 'CHANGE'` and `context_id: {changeEventId}` — the data is persisted, just not surfaced in any history UI
- `ChangeAssessment` stores metadata (status, impact, notes, AI analysis) — can be the index for the timeline
- Current gap: assessment chats don't appear in the regular chat history sidebar, making them effectively invisible after the session ends
