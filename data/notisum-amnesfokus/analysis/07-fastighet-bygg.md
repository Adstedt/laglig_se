# Fastighet och byggande -- Laglistaanalys

## 1. Overview

This list contains **110 documents** governing property law, building regulation, construction safety, energy performance, and facility management in Sweden. All documents are currently placed under a single UNCATEGORIZED section (section 00) with no internal structure. The collection is unusually broad, spanning classical property law dating back to 1736, modern building codes, workplace-safety prescriptions for construction sites, energy-efficiency mandates, environmental waste rules, and specialised authority prescriptions from Boverket, Lantmateriet, MSB, Elsakhetsverket, and others.

### Key characteristics

- Largest single-list inventory among the laglistor analysed so far (110 documents)
- No existing section structure at all -- every document sits in section 00
- Boverket is the dominant authority (18 BFS documents), reflecting BBR and its 2024 breakout prescriptions
- Strong overlap with environmental lists (14 shared with Miljo, 7 with Miljo for tjansteforetag) and workplace-safety lists (9 shared with Arbetsmiljo)
- Includes five AFS prescriptions specifically for construction-site safety
- Several very old statutes: Jordabalk (1970), Byggningabalk (1736), Samaaganderattslag (1904)
- BBR transition in progress: both the consolidated BBR (BFS 2011:6) and the individual replacement prescriptions (BFS 2024:4-13) appear in the list

## 4. Content Pattern Assessment

### Structural patterns

- **Triplet stacking:** Three major compliance areas follow a law + ordinance + Boverket prescription pattern: energy declarations (SFS 2006:985 + SFS 2006:1592 + BFS 2007:4), energy measurement (SFS 2022:333 + SFS 2022:336 + BFS 2022:3), and climate declarations (SFS 2021:787 + SFS 2021:789 + BFS 2021:7)
- **BBR fragmentation:** The list includes both the consolidated BBR (BFS 2011:6) and seven individual replacement prescriptions (BFS 2024:4-13), reflecting an active regulatory transition
- **Historical layering:** Property-law instruments span nearly 300 years (1736-2024), creating a uniquely deep temporal range
- **AFS construction cluster:** All five AFS prescriptions relate specifically to construction-site safety (Bas-P/Bas-U, scaffolding, workplace design), forming a self-contained sub-list

### Unique characteristics

- This is the only laglista with zero EU instruments -- all obligations flow through Swedish national law
- The dual AFS 2023:3 entries represent different chapters of the same prescription (a scraping artifact)
- Section I contains several instruments with opaque short titles (e.g., SFS 1985:658, SFS 1993:527) that need verification against lagrummet.se for proper identification

### Gap analysis

- **No coverage of Lag om skydd mot olyckor (SFS 2003:778)** -- the overarching civil protection act that governs fire-safety duties for property owners is absent from this list despite being directly relevant
- **No coverage of Stralskyddsforordningen (SFS 2018:506)** -- the radiation-protection ordinance is absent though the Stralskyddslag is present
- **Limited environmental-permit coverage** -- Miljobalken is not referenced despite its relevance to building on contaminated land
- **No coverage of Lag om tillganglighetsanpassning av viss samhallsservice** -- accessibility obligations for commercial premises are addressed only at the BFS prescription level

## 5. Laglig.se Recommendations

### Section naming strategy

Use the laglig.se section names proposed in Section 2 above:

1. **Fastighetsratt och agande** (core property law)
2. **Bostadsratt, hyra och forvaltning** (housing tenure and management)
3. **Plan- och byggregler** (planning and building framework)
4. **Boverkets byggnadsforeskrifter** (detailed building-performance rules)
5. **Byggnadsarbetsmiljo och sakerhet** (construction-site safety)
6. **Energi, klimat och installationer** (energy, climate, electrical)
7. **Miljo, avfall och halsa i byggnader** (environmental management for buildings)
8. **Brand, sotning och raddning** (fire protection)
9. **Kulturmiljo, offentlig upphandling och ovriga regler** (heritage, procurement, misc.)

### Compliance summary approach

For the property/construction audience, frame compliance obligations around three user roles:

1. **Fastighetsagare** -- Sections A, B, F, G, H are primary (ownership, tenure, energy, environment, fire)
2. **Byggherre/entreprenor** -- Sections C, D, E are primary (planning, building codes, construction safety)
3. **Forvaltare** -- Sections B, D, F, G, H are primary (housing management, building performance, energy, environment, fire)

### Priority ordering for agents

When an agent identifies a user as a property owner, present sections in this order:

1. Section A (property rights -- fundamental legal position)
2. Section C (planning and building -- for any construction project)
3. Section F (energy -- mandatory declarations and EV charging)
4. Section G (environment -- waste, radon, PCB, water quality)
5. Section H (fire -- ongoing safety obligations)
6. Section B (housing -- if managing rental or bostadsratt)
7. Section D (Boverket prescriptions -- for new construction or renovation)
8. Section E (construction safety -- if acting as byggherre)
9. Section I (heritage, procurement -- contextual)

### BBR transition guidance

- Flag BFS 2011:6 (BBR) as "being replaced by BFS 2024:4 through BFS 2024:13, effective 1 July 2025"
- Present the individual BFS 2024 prescriptions alongside their BBR predecessor sections for cross-reference
- Once the transition completes, consider removing BFS 2011:6 or marking it as superseded

| Informationssakerhet Sverige | 0           | No direct overlap                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| Halsa och sjukvard           | 0           | No direct overlap (though HSLF-FS 2024:33 on legionella could be shared in the future)                                                                                                                       |

**Key insight for laglig.se:** Property owners and byggherrar need this list in combination with the Arbetsmiljo list for complete construction-project compliance. The environmental overlap is substantial -- consider cross-referencing Sections G and H with the Miljo list.
