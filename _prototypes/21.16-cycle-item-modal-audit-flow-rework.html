What changes (numbered)

① Section reorder (left panel)

Anmärkningar → Kravpunkter → Hur påverkar → Underlag
Hur påverkar → Kravpunkter → Underlag → Anmärkningar

Matches the auditor's mental flow: understand → review → check → record. Findings emerge from the review, not as the entry point.

② Lagansvarig in header

7 identical "AA" avatars (one per kravpunkt row)
Single "Lagansvarig: Alexander Adstedt" stripe at the top.

Per-row avatar only when the kravpunkt has an explicit assignee that differs from the lagansvarig (orange "AB" in the mockup). Cuts noise; surfaces actual delegation.

③ Agree-or-dispute primitive + honest "Att uppmärksamma"

"Granskningsstatus" with fake "X av Y kravpunkter granskade" — system doesn't track per-krav review
Signera card leads with "Nuvarande status i laglistan: X — stämmer det?" + Bekräfta/Avvik buttons. "Att uppmärksamma" box surfaces only honest signals (bevis gaps, open findings, last-reviewed timestamp).

The actual audit primitive is agree or dispute the team's claim, not per-kravpunkt review. Bekräfta = 2-click happy path (auto-fills bedömning + motivering). Avvik = forces real explanation.

Not in this prototype — the larger items (#4 audit-flow stepper, "Anmärkning vs Motivering" copy hint, structured auditor checklist). Those need design conversations before implementation; the three above are mechanical and high-payoff.
