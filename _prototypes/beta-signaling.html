Recommendation

  • Badge: B — Sage accent, copy = Beta. On-brand (uses the secondary sage), visible without shouting, short enough not to crowd the wordmark. Same pill in light + dark.
  • Banner: A — thin sage top strip, marketing pages only, dismissible (localStorage-once, same pattern as the cookie banner). Quiet, on-brand, gets out of the way after one read.
  • In-app: badge only — no banner. Combination shown in §4.
  • Both link to feedback — the badge to a feedback route/mailto, the banner says it explicitly. Beta's whole point is signal collection.
  • One flag — NEXT_PUBLIC_BETA_MODE + a BETA_END_DATE constant so it self-expires after the four weeks. Sunset = flip one value.
