What changed · row

One-line row, no accordion. Inline signals under the law name summarize the contents: colored dots for open findings (severity color), muted counts for artefakter/kravpunkter. User scans a 20-row list and knows where to click without expanding anything.

What changed · drawer

Slide-in from the right, 560 px. Table stays anchored behind it so the user keeps their place. Findings lead the stack (highest-signal content), Kravpunkter + Artefakter follow. Stängda findings are collapsed by default — audit trail preserved but not in the way.

What changed · findings

Severity-first ordering (Avvikelse MAJOR → MINOR → Observation → Förbättring). Each card: left-rail color, type pill, title with description preview, metadata line (assignee + due date + task link). Clicks through to the finding editor. No more color-soup chip row.
