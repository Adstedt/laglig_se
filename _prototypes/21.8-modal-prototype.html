Why modal, not drawer

Drawer occupies the right edge — collision course with future AI-chat surface. Modal sits centered, leaving the whole viewport behind it undisturbed. Also reuses the existing SplitPanelModal shell already powering the law-list-item modal — zero new pattern.

AI chat lives inside the modal

Click Fråga Lexa in the right panel. The right panel collapses, chat slides in at 3fr, rail shows the essentials (bedömning, ansvarig, finding count) at 44 px on the far right. Kravpunkter + findings stay visible in the left panel — Lexa has their context without stealing their real-estate.

Layout rhythm

State 1: left 3fr (findings + kravpunkter + artefakter) · right 2fr (details, motivering, snabblänkar, hälsa).
State 2: left 3fr · chat 3fr · rail 44 px. Right panel is always mounted — just hidden via CSS, so SWR caches and optimistic state survive the transition.
