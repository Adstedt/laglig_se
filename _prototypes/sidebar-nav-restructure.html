Open questions
Q1
Is Krav tier-gated too, or free? If Kontroller is PRO, Krav probably is too — they share the kravpunkter substrate.
Q2
Should Ändringsbevakning stay in Arbetsyta or live closer to Ändringar in Regelverk? The latter groups "law-change feed + my subs" semantically.
Q3
AUDITOR role sees reduced surface — does the sidebar adapt per role, or do disabled items show with a lock icon? (Story 21.14 implication)
Q4
When tier is below PRO, does Kontroller hide entirely, show with an upsell badge, or stay clickable into a paywall page? Last option doubles as marketing surface.
