# Rutin (procedure)

A rutin describes HOW the company performs a recurring process — who does what,
when, in which order. It operationalises a policy.

## Structure

1. **Syfte och omfattning** — which process, which situations trigger it, who it
   applies to.
2. **Ansvar** — roles in the process (utförare, kontrollant, dokumentansvarig).
3. **Genomförande** — the process steps in order, numbered (`orderedList`) or as
   sub-headings per phase. Each step: who does it + what is done + what is
   documented.
4. **Avvikelser** — what happens when the rutin cannot be followed (vem kontaktas,
   hur dokumenteras avvikelsen).
5. **Dokumentation och uppföljning** — which records the rutin produces, where they
   are stored, and how often the rutin is reviewed.

## Style

- Process voice, present tense per role: "Närmaste chef anmäler…",
  "HR dokumenterar…". Steps may be imperative when addressing the performer.
- Concrete triggers and time bounds ("inom 24 timmar", "senast den 31 januari").

## Criteria

- Genomförande has clearly ordered steps, each with an actor (role) — a rutin
  without WHO is not actionable.
- Avvikelsehantering is present.
- States which dokumentation each critical step leaves behind (spårbarhet).
- Governing law for the process is cited where it dictates a step (GR-001).
