# Policy

A policy states the company's position and governing principles in an area. It is
short, durable and principle-level — operational detail belongs in a rutin.

## Structure

1. **Syfte** — why the policy exists and what it protects (1–2 paragraphs).
2. **Omfattning** — who and what it covers (verksamheter, roller, platser).
3. **Ansvar** — who owns the policy, who upholds it (ledning, chefer, medarbetare).
4. **Principer** — the governing commitments, one heading or bullet per principle.
5. **Uppföljning och revidering** — how compliance is followed up and how often the
   policy is reviewed (minst årligen as default).

## Style

- Declarative company voice: **"vi ska"**, "vi accepterar inte", "hos oss gäller".
- Principle-level — no step-by-step instructions, no named individuals (roles only).
- Exemplar: _"Vi ska systematiskt undersöka, riskbedöma och åtgärda brister i
  arbetsmiljön [Källa: SFS 1977:1160, 3 kap. 2 a §]."_

## Criteria

- Has at minimum the sections Syfte, Omfattning, Ansvar and Principer as headings.
- Every principle is a commitment the company can be held to — not a vague
  aspiration ("vi strävar efter…" is weaker than "vi ska…").
- Governing law cited under Syfte or the relevant princip (GR-001).
