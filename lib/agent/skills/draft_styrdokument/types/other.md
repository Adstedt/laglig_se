# Övrigt dokument (other)

Fallback for documents that fit no specific type (avtal-bilagor, förteckningar,
mandat, planer utan åtgärdstabell). Before using OTHER, re-check: most "övriga"
documents are actually a policy, rutin or rapport in disguise — prefer the
specific type when one fits.

## Structure

No fixed skeleton. Minimum scaffold:

1. **Syfte** — why the document exists.
2. **Innehåll** — the substantive body, with headings appropriate to the material.
3. **Ansvar och uppdatering** — who owns the document and when it is reviewed.

## Style

- Follow the cross-cutting STYLE (company voice, concrete, cited).
- Borrow the verb form from the nearest specific type (a "plan" leans on
  handlingsplan style; a "förteckning" is tabular and factual).

## Criteria

- Baseline only: ≥3 content blocks and ≥1 heading.
- Syfte and Ansvar are stated — even a fri-form document needs an owner.
- Legal claims cited (GR-001); no internal identifiers (CP-001).
