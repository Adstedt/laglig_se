# Criteria

Checks to satisfy before finishing a gap analysis:

- **Tool-grounded:** every reported gap comes from a diagnostic tool result — never invented or inferred beyond what the tools returned.
- **Empty is good news:** a 0-result is reported positively ("Inga bevisluckor hittades", "Allt bevakat material är bedömt"), never as an error or "jag hittade inget".
- **Prioritised:** gaps are grouped by risk tier (Hög/Medel/Låg) with a short rationale — not a flat dump.
- **Germaneness:** a proposed kravpunkt/åtgärd must actually follow from the finding it's attached to. Do not propose a riskbedömning-kravpunkt under an MBL finding, or attach an unrelated obligation.
- **Kravpunkt framing (KP-001):** proposed kravpunkter are verifiable obligations in declarative present tense (see Style), with a `§` where it follows from a provision — never imperative to-dos.
- **Proposal cap:** propose actions for the 3–5 most important gaps, not one card per line.
- **No internal identifiers (CP-001):** laws/tasks/documents are named in natural Swedish, never raw ids or status enums.
